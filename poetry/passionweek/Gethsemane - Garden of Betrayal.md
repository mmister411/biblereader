# Gethsemane - Garden of Betrayal

A quiet had fell on the grass and the trees.
For a moment in time there was still and serene.
Then the silence was dashed by a murmuring horde
bearing lanterns to search, armed with bludgeons and swords.

Every flame from each torch flickers fast through the air.
Through the lights you can see that Judas was there.
The chief priests sent in guards to assure apprehension
of one who kept preaching of love and redemption.

The betrayer stepped forward as part of the plan.
Only Judas knew clearly which one was the man.
He calls "Rabbi" with hope of disguising his aims.
He prepares for the signal of who they should claim.

He leans in with a kiss placed on Jesus' cheek
to alert to the guards of the one that they seek.
“You betray Son of Man with a kiss?” Jesus asked.
“Hurry up with it then.  Carry on with your task.”
With no sign of remorse Judas slinks to the side,
having finished assisting this turning of tides.
 
The disciples were shocked by the sudden suspense.
They then asked “Master, how should we mount a defense?”
In the blink of an eye without time left to think
you could hear Peter’s sword with a shing and a clink.
Then a swoosh and a shrill and an uncanny sound
Of an ear falling off and then splatting the ground.
 
The air thickened with tension for that man’s position. 
He’s named Malchus and serves as the High Priest’s assistant
This attack very likely affords a death sentence.
Then Jesus said “STOP THIS” responding that instant.

“Put your sword in its place” spoken firmly with grace
While returning the ear up to Malchus’ face.
“If you live by the sword then by sword you will fall ”
Can I not seek my Father with Angels on Call?
But if legions of Angels descend in full force 
How could scripture fulfillment keep running its course?” 

Jesus turned to the crowd “have you come here with blades
And with clubs, spears and staves. Why are you so afraid?”
Of a robber? I taught in your courts every day
Not so much as an elbow was raised in my way.
In reality darkness is flexing its power
Therefore now you’re emboldened to act in this hour.”
His words hung in the air woven thick as a quilt 
Falling firmly on fears and unspoken guilt.

He continued on “Who have you come here to find?”
“One called Jesus of Nazareth” someone replied.
“I am He” Jesus said and not a soul was prepared.
The earth quaked, the ground shaking, they fell over scared.

Though confused while they stumbled they returned to their feet.
Jesus said once again to them “Whom do you seek?”
With attempts to regain their composure and pride 
 “One called Jesus of Nazareth” someone replied.
“I am He”  Jesus said and his power released
That most ancient of sound permeated the ground
Every knee buckled down and all confidence ceased.
Barely able to cope they return to their feet.

The disciples were shook as they  scatter in dread.
Jesus stands undeterred while abandonment spreads.
He told them “strike the shepherd the sheep would disperse”
But would also lose none, per the chapter and verse.

Guards began seizing Jesus to carry him off.
A young man followed close in a burial cloth.
He was not their before and the guards were amazed
since he dressed like he’d stood up and walked out a grave.

So the Guards tried to nab this mystery man 
But could only grab cloth with how quickly he ran.
They recenter their focus with Jesus in hand
As they claim their success in their shadowy plan
