# Chapter 1: Unashamed of the Gospel

FFrom a servant of Jesus, Apostle of Christ
named Paul, set apart with the gospel in sight.
This same sort of gospel in prophesy past
was foretold as is written, now here at long last. 
The prophets foretold about David's offspring, 
That would be Son of GOD which is Jesus our King. 

So how do we know it is Jesus that's Christ? 
Through the Spirit of holiness flowing His might 
resurrecting our LORD and declaring Him right. 
 From this Christ our apostleship stems and is based.
By His grace we go out to each nation and place.

In each nation we labor to cultivate faith
In His name that's obedient, powered by grace.
By which grace you were called, and with others the same
For the sake of His wonderful glorious name.

Now with that out the way I would now like to say,
To the saints there in Rome: grace and peace now this day. 
Grace and peace go to you and I hope all is right.: 
From GOD and our Father and our LORD Jesus Christ 
 
So first I must say I am thankful for you, 
unto GOD through our Christ for His goodness you do. 
All the world mentions you that your faith it is true. 
With my witness as GOD whom my spirit does serve
in the gospel of Christ as His worth does deserve; 
I mention you when I pray, unceasing with words. 
 
I've been making requests and to pray as to say: 
That by GOD's will my journey may bring me your way. 
For so long I've desired to see you once more 
To impart to you spiritual gifts from the LORD. 
So that you may be strong even more than before. 
Which in turn we  may comfort each other in grace.
As to help and support by our mutual faiths.
 
On this subject about my soon travels to you, 
I don't want you to guess about previous clues. 
In regards to before I quite surely desired
to be there with you but my options expired 
As I've been working so that the gentiles bear fruit. 
For I am a debtor to Jew, Greek, and brute. 
To Barbarians, unwise, as well as astute. 
 
Therefore I digress and am eager to preach 
To each and every Roman I'd possibly reach. 
For I am unashamed of the Gospel of Christ 
Since it's the power of GOD to deliver us life. 
For all who believe, which is first for the Jew.
Then believers that come from the rest of us too. 
 
For in this righteousness of our GOD is revealed. 
From His faith to our faith, as is written and sealed: 
"the righteous shall live by faith" so says His word. 
For from Heaven the Wrath of our GOD is made clear 
as firmly against all the worldliness here
And all wrong doings people do; both old and youth; 
That through their wrong doing try hiding the truth. 
 
For GOD shows of Himself what they can surely know. 
In a way that is clear that they can not forego 
For since all the world was created and made 
His invisible traits were so neatly arrayed. 
Fingerprinted throughout all the things that were made.
So that all their excuses just fizzle and fade. 
 
Even so with their knowledge of GOD set in place, 
They gave him no glory as GOD, not a trace. 
They gave Him no thanks and their minds were laid waist. 
Self proclaimed to be wise but turned foolish in heart 
And more dark than the blackest of parts of deep space. 
 
The glory of GOD they just swapped and exchanged 
For the glory of man which is dark and deranged. 
They exchanged the immortal for things that can break. 
For birds and for snakes and for things GOD creates. 
 
With this reasoning GOD takes His hands off the wheel. 
lets them have what they wish, gives them up to their ills. 
They're released to their lusts and their hearts ever black.
Who loved Lies over GOD and their lusts over facts. 
Who worship things; not Him, whom is why things begin 
Namely GOD, who is bles'sed forever, Amen. 
 
So the LORD takes a step as if out of the way. 
As corrupted affections begin their decay. 
For example their women leave nature behind. 
They give into lusts of unnatural kinds. 
And the men follow suit and leave women behind 
as they lust for each other and think that it's fine.
They'll receive the results of their error in time. 
 
Since they deemed it as needless to hold what they knew
of GOD, He released them to follow it through.
They’re released to a mind that is broken and dazed.
They're turned loose to indulge in disordered ways.
 
Being filled with unrighteousness, malice and greed,
fornication and wickedness, envy, deceit.
They slander in secret and boast in their pride.
They're defiant of parents, respect left aside.

They invent what is evil, lack wisdom and care,
unfaithful, unloving, no mercy to spare.
They know God’s decree on the deeds they defend,
that death is the wage at the road’s bitter end,
yet and still they persist while their fellows commend.
